About VATI

At VATI, trainings are the core of our activity. We ensure they are at the top to push each trainee further and make them fully understand the objectives future airlines might ask them before entering their airline.

All our courses are divided into 2 or more Modules which are all essential for the success of the cadet in passing his formation at our school. All our courses are supervised and structured by Certified Instructors which teach the material cadets need to learn in order to obtain their diploma.

Each course has 1 final exam reviewing everything that was covered in previous Modules. This final exam will always be evaluated by the Head Instructor which would then give a mark out of 50 to the cadet. Depending on this grade, you will be awarded their diploma if you have achieved the required grade or scored above.

2 Programs within our School

APC

Airline Pilot Certification

Professional Pilot Training

  • APC-101 Theoretical work. (Taxiing, taking-off, landings, VFR, IFR, ILS, PAPI, etc...)

Airline Pilot Certification (APC)

Professional Pilot Training Program

Program Overview

Composed of 3 components, you will first dive into theoretical work before putting your knowledge to test within PTFS on airliners. Finally, you will have to pass one final exam to receive your diploma from our school.

Obtaining your APC can also be of great use when applying at PTC airlines.

APC-101: Theoretical Work

Master the foundational knowledge of flight operations including taxiing, taking-off, landings, VFR, IFR, ILS, PAPI, and more. This module covers all the essential theory you need to become a proficient pilot.

APC-201: Practical Work

Everything learned in class put into practice in-game. Apply your theoretical knowledge in real flight scenarios within PTFS, operating airliners under various conditions.

APC-301: Final Examination

Review and final examination course covering all aspects of your training. Successfully pass this comprehensive exam to receive your APC diploma.

Cabin Crew Certification (CCC)

Professional Cabin Service Training

Program Overview

Composed of 2 components, the Cabin Crew Certification will make you practice daily work methods and ethics of Cabin Crews, but also how to react in emergency situations.

This program will have you well prepared for future PTC airlines you wish to apply at.

CCC-101: Cabin Crew Duties & Emergency Response

Learn essential cabin crew duties, professional service standards, and comprehensive emergency response procedures. Master the skills needed to ensure passenger safety and comfort.

CCC-201: Review & Final Examination

Comprehensive review and final examination course covering all aspects of cabin crew operations. Demonstrate your mastery to earn your certification.
