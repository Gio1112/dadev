Cabin baggage tips

  • Keep liquids in containers up to 100 ml and place them in a transparent bag where required.
  • Store medicines, travel documents, and valuables in your carry-on items.
  • Make sure your cabin bag fits overhead-bin dimensions on your route.

Checked baggage tips

  • Attach contact details to every checked bag and keep your baggage receipt until arrival.
  • Avoid packing restricted and fragile items in checked baggage.
  • Use approved protective packaging for sports equipment and special items.

Baggage FAQ

Can I buy extra baggage later?

Yes, extra baggage can usually be added online in Manage booking before departure.

What if my baggage is delayed?

Please submit a delayed-baggage report at the arrival airport and keep your baggage tags for reference.

Do carry-on limits differ by aircraft?

Cabin baggage dimensions and number of items can vary by route and fare, so always review your ticket details.
