How Avios work

Collect Avios from flights and partners, then redeem them for award flights, upgrades, and selected travel extras.

Tier points

Tier points are earned from eligible flights and help you progress through membership levels for added travel benefits.

Ways to redeem

Use Avios for award flights, cabin upgrades, selected extras, and partner benefits depending on availability.

How tiers work

Tier points reset on your membership anniversary. Maintaining tier benefits requires reaching the threshold each period.

Family sharing

Household and family features may help combine earned value, subject to program terms and account setup.

Estimated accrual at booking

You can view estimated Avios during booking and compare fares based on expected loyalty accrual.
